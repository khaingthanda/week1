University of Yangon

ADIMISSION

Students who have had the High School Completion Certificate and passed the Matriculation Examination and who apply for admission to University of Yangon
are selected by the University on the basis of aggregate marks and on principles laid down by the University Council.

REQUIREMENTS FOR THE FIRST DEGREE

Specialization begins from the First Year of the studies. Duration for the first degree is four years (Law degree is five years). There are two semesters
in each academic year (December – March and June – September) examinations are held at the end of each semester. A student is expected to complete a specific class at
most within two consecutive academic years. Otherwise, he or she will be permitted to repeat the same examination once more as an external student only in the
following academic year. Fourth year students are required to sit for both written and viva voce examinations. In addition, they will be required to submit a term-
paper. Students are required to obtain a minimum of Grade Point 3 in each subject. The grading system of undergraduate degree courses and Honours degree
courses is as follows. The duration of degree programme is as follows:

  • First degree 4 years
  • Honours degree 5 years
  • Master degree 3 years
  • Graduate Diploma 2 years(ELT Diploma-One Year)
  • Doctoral degree 5 years
